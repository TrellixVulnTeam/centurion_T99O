Advanced Technology Brief:

Multilingual Conversational User Interface

  What I Will Show You Today
  • A prototype example of a conversational user interface - a chat bot
    • Cloud-hosted artifical intelligence to determine intent and guide conversations with customers
    • On-demand translation of more than 100 languages, automatically detected
    • Interaction through typed text or spoken word
    • Responsive design - can run on PC, Kiosk, tablet, or phone

  • Prototype implements two use cases:
    • New Application: Collects customer information, validates documents & completes applicaiton
    • Revised Application: Looks up existing customer information & revises application
    • Both use case may be executed by either speech or text in English or by text in any other supported language
  Why I Am Showing You This
  • SSA field offices must service large numbers of non-English speaking customers. We would like to make it easier to:
    • Interact with them in their native language when possible
    • Automatically perform a cursory validation of a customer's supporting documents
    • Use the information gathered in the conversation to fill out an applicaiton

  • Make productive use of the customer's time spent on line

  • Maximize the value of the time spent in human interaction
